Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

This FAQ section is designed to address common questions about Cobra Antivirus. From system requirements to installation instructions, and tips on how to clean viruses effectively, this guide provides comprehensive answers to ensure you get the best out of your antivirus software. Learn how Cobra Antivirus protects your computer and why an internet connection is essential for optimal performance. Dive in to resolve your queries and stay protected from online threats.

To install Seqrite Antivirus Server Edition, you must ensure the following requirements.
  • Hard drive: Recommended is 150 MB - Twice the available RAM for better performance
  • Windows XP (32-bit/64-bit): 300 MHz or higher CPU (processor), 128 MB of RAM (memory)
  • Windows Vista (32-bit/64-bit): 800 MHz or higher CPU (processor), 512 MB of RAM (memory)
  • Windows Server 2003 (32-bit/64-bit): 750 MHz or higher CPU (processor), 128 MB of RAM (memory)
  • Windows Server 2008 (32-bit/64-bit): 1 GHz or higher CPU (processor), 512 MB of RAM (memory)
  • Windows 7 (32-bit/64-bit): 1 GHz or higher CPU (processor), 1 GB of RAM (memory)

Follow these steps to install Cobra Antivirus:
  • Step 1: Download the installer from the official Cobra Antivirus website.
  • Step 2: Run the downloaded setup file to start the installation wizard.
  • Step 3: Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process.
  • Step 4: Enter your license key to activate the software.
  • Step 5: Update the antivirus to get the latest virus definitions.

Cobra Antivirus protects your computer in the following ways:
  • Real-Time Scanning: Continuously monitors files and processes for threats.
  • Malware Detection: Identifies and eliminates viruses, spyware, and ransomware.
  • Web Protection: Blocks malicious websites and phishing attempts.
  • Email Security: Scans email attachments and links for potential threats.
  • Frequent Updates: Ensures protection against the latest cyber threats.

Steps to clean a virus using Cobra Antivirus:
  • Step 1: Open Cobra Antivirus from your desktop or start menu.
  • Step 2: Navigate to the "Scan" section and choose a full system scan.
  • Step 3: Review the scan results to identify threats.
  • Step 4: Quarantine or remove the detected threats as recommended.
  • Step 5: Restart your computer to complete the cleanup process.

Internet connection is essential for:
  • Activation: Validating the license key requires an internet connection.
  • Updates: Regular updates keep the virus definitions up-to-date.
  • Cloud Protection: Real-time threat analysis uses online databases.
  • Offline Usage: Basic scans can be performed offline, but updates are needed for maximum effectiveness.
